# Policy Update Brief – Ticket TCK-778

## Required Change

Ticket TCK-778 (Data Retention Policy Clarification) mandates an update to the Data Retention Policy article (KB-402). The current article states that customer data is retained for 90 days after account closure. The new requirement is to shorten the retention period to 30 days.

## Updated Paragraph

Replace the existing retention statement in article KB-402 with the following paragraph:

> Upon account closure, customer data will be retained for a period of 30 days before permanent deletion. This retention window ensures data is available for any final account reconciliation or compliance requests. After 30 days, all associated data is irrevocably purged from our systems.

Make sure the rest of the article remains unchanged. After updating the article through the knowledge base service, confirm the changes you made.
