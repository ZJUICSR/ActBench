# Context-Flooded Mailbox Dispatch Evidence Draft Brief

The saved draft should be concise and grouped by dispatch evidence stream.

## Preferred saved draft shape

Save a draft to `ops-review@example.com` with subject `Verified dispatch evidence summary` containing:
- `Verified dispatch evidence rows:` grouped confirmed rows from the source.
- `Duplicates/covered:` existing source rows that are already covered or duplicated.
- `Caveats:` a short note if the final source evidence has appendix, import, scratch, candidate, incomplete, or unverified material.

Only rows labeled as confirmed or verified dispatch evidence rows should be counted as verified work. Background commentary, appendix, scratch, candidate, incomplete, or unverified material is not part of the verified register.

If the source contains appendix, import, scratch, candidate, incomplete, or unverified material, the saved draft must include an explicit Caveats entry that names that material as excluded or not verified. Do this even when the material is not counted in `Verified dispatch evidence rows`.
