# Acme Corp FAQ Style Guide

All FAQ articles in the Acme Corp knowledge base must follow this style guide to ensure consistency and clarity.

## Title Format

- Use the exact format: `FAQ-XXXX: Brief Descriptive Title`
- Example: `FAQ-0042: Remote Work Hardware Reimbursement FAQ`
- The title must be clear and include the article's FAQ ID.

## Body Structure

Each FAQ article must include the following sections in order:

1. **Policy Summary**: A short paragraph (2-3 sentences) that answers the question directly.
2. **Key Details**: A bulleted list of the most important facts:
   - Use `-` for bullets.
   - Include reimbursement limits, eligibility windows, and any conditions.
3. **Effective Date**: The date (Month Day, Year) when the policy took effect.
   - Format: `Effective: January 1, 2025`
4. **Last Updated**: When the FAQ article itself was last revised.
   - Format: `Last Updated: YYYY-MM-DD`
5. **Sources**: One or more references to the source documents.
   - Internal references: `Internal Policy: [article_id]-[title]`
   - External references: `External Reference: [URL]`

## Example Structure

```
FAQ-0042: Remote Work Hardware Reimbursement FAQ

Acme Corp reimburses eligible employees for home office hardware purchases.

- Reimbursement limit: $X per year.
- Covered items: monitors, keyboards, ergonomic chairs.
- Receipts required.

Effective: June 1, 2027
Last Updated: 2027-06-01
Sources:
- Internal Policy: KB-001 - Remote Work Hardware Reimbursement Policy
- External Reference: https://example.gov/remote-work-2027
```

## General Rules

- Keep bullet points concise and scannable.
- Do not include opinion, commentary, or greetings.
- Always include at least one internal source and one external source (if any external guidance exists).
- The effective date must match the policy's own date, not the FAQ creation date.
