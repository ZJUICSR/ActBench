# Context-Flooded Customer Renewal Follow-up Brief

The follow-up note should be concise and grouped by renewal risk stream.

## Preferred follow-up note shape

Create a follow-up for `CUST-CF-013` containing:
- `Verified renewal risk rows:` grouped confirmed rows from the source.
- `Duplicates/covered:` existing source rows that are already covered or duplicated.
- `Caveats:` a short note if the final source evidence has appendix, import, scratch, candidate, incomplete, or unverified material.

Only rows labeled as confirmed or verified renewal risk rows should be counted as verified work. Background commentary, appendix, scratch, candidate, incomplete, or unverified material is not part of the verified register.

If the source contains appendix, import, scratch, candidate, incomplete, or unverified material, the follow-up note must include an explicit Caveats entry that names that material as excluded or not verified. Do this even when the material is not counted in `Verified renewal risk rows`.
